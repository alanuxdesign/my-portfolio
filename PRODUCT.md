# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters and hiring managers evaluating Alan for engineering roles. They arrive with limited time, often between meetings or mid-pipeline, scanning for proof of craft, range, and judgment. Desktop-first during work hours; mobile is secondary but must not feel broken.

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio (`my-portfolio`) that makes hiring decisions easier by surfacing selected projects and case studies first. Success looks like: a recruiter understands what Alan builds, clicks into 2–3 representative projects, and leaves with confidence to reach out or advance the candidacy. The site is the product; there is no separate app shell behind it.

## Brand Personality

Warm, craft-forward, human. Product-marketing polish at the level of intentional surfaces (Vercel, Raycast) without becoming a SaaS landing page. Voice is approachable and specific, not stiff résumé copy. Emotional goal: trust plus curiosity — "this person cares about details and ships real UI."

## Anti-references

- Obvious template or create-next-app starter (Geist boilerplate, unchanged zinc palette, default "Deploy Now" hero patterns)
- Generic portfolio filler that could belong to anyone
- SaaS landing clichés if the site grows (hero metrics, identical icon-card grids, purple-gradient dark mode)

## Design Principles

1. **Show work before telling** — lead with projects; headlines and intros earn their space.
2. **Craft is credibility** — spacing, type, and motion should feel intentional on every viewport.
3. **Human warmth over corporate polish** — copy and layout stay approachable, not enterprise-stiff.
4. **Product-quality marketing surfaces** — Vercel/Raycast-level intentionality without template energy.
5. **Accessible by default** — WCAG 2.2 AA baseline; push to AAA where straightforward (contrast, focus, motion).

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.2 AA as the floor; target AAA for contrast, focus visibility, and touch targets where it does not compromise the design. Honor `prefers-reduced-motion` strictly. Keyboard navigation and visible focus states are required on all interactive project and navigation patterns.
